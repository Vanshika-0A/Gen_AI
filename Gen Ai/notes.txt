What is a word Embedding?
-Dense vector representation of a word that captures meaning

TF-IDF treats words a independent tokens
RF-IDF does not understand:
car = automobile
mobile = electronics
happy = joyful

So we need a way to represent words based on meaning, not just count

Technique for dense numerical vectors:
Word2Vec
- one of the technique in NLP used to represent words as dense numerical vector
-Developed by Google in 2013
-it allows machine to understand the semantic relationship between words by positioning similar words closer together in a high-dimentional vector space



Core idea behind Word2Vec
-Words that appear in similar context have similar meanings
-This is called:Distributional Hypothesis


Core Architecture:
-CBOW -> Continuous Bag of Words
     -Model that predicts a target word based on its surrounding context words.

-Skip-gram
        -using a single word to predict the surrounding context words.It is fast for infrquent words

CBOW: Predict target word from surrounding context
Input: I am learning _________ intelligence 
prediction: artificial


Skip-Gram: Predict surrounding words from a target word
Input: intelligence
Prediction: artificial,human,machine


key differences b/w Sparse Matrix and Dense Matrix
1.  Sparse - Mostly zero values
    Dense - Most values are meaningful, non-zero values

2. Memory efficiency 
Sparse - Highly memory-efficient for large datasets
Dense - Memory intensive for large datasets
